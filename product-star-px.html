What you're actually buying

A PCP stores compressed air in an onboard reservoir, so firing is just a valve opening — zero recoil, zero cocking effort, zero hold-sensitivity. The Star PX gives you ninety of those shots per fill, fed from a 10-round magazine, through a two-stage trigger you can actually stage.

The integrated suppressor takes the crack off each shot — your neighbours hear a stapler, not a rifle — and at 2.45 kg it's the lightest full-power rifle we sell. Fill it from a PCP hand pump (see the PXi Combo if you don't own one yet) and the air is literally free.

Best for: precision shooters stepping up from springers, 25 m+ target work, and anyone who wants magazine-fed follow-up shots with match-grade consistency.
