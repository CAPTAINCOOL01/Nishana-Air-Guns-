Our story

Built for the Indian shooter.

Nishana was started because buying an airgun in India shouldn't feel like a treasure hunt. Between grey-market marketplaces, unclear licensing rules, and dealers who ghost you after payment — most first-time buyers give up before they ever put a pellet through paper.

So we built a shop that carries only licence-exempt airguns, gives you a GST invoice and manufacturer warranty on every unit, and lets you talk to a human on WhatsApp before and after you buy.

We now focus on one airgun by design: the Star RX Gen 3. That lets our team give useful answers about its magazines, CO₂, pellets, setup, running costs and ownership instead of offering a long, shallow catalogue.

The legal bit — in plain English

The Star RX Gen 3 configuration Nishana sells is .177" (4.5 mm) and approximately 3 joules. It falls within category III(f)(ii) of India's Arms Rules 2016 and is licence-exempt; the seller must still verify the buyer's identity and address, and local restrictions can apply. We do not sell airguns that require an individual arms licence. Read our sourced legal guide or talk to our sales team before ordering.
