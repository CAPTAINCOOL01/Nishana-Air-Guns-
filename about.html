Our story

Built for the Indian shooter.

Nishana was started because buying an airgun in India shouldn't feel like a treasure hunt. Between grey-market marketplaces, unclear licensing rules, and dealers who ghost you after payment — most first-time buyers give up before they ever put a pellet through paper.

So we built a shop that carries only licence-exempt airguns, gives you a GST invoice and manufacturer warranty on every unit, and lets you talk to a human on WhatsApp before and after you buy.

We're multi-brand by design — Camstar, Precihole, SDB and more — because you shouldn't have to browse five separate sites to compare a rifle you actually want.

The legal bit — in plain English

Under India's Arms Rules 2016, airguns with a muzzle energy of 20 joules or less, in .177" (4.5 mm) calibre, are exempt from licensing. Every product Nishana sells sits inside that exemption. We do not sell to buyers under 18, and we do not sell any airgun that requires a licence. If you have a question about the legal status of a specific product, ask us on WhatsApp before ordering — we'd rather over-explain than have you own something you shouldn't.
