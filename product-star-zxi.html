Frequently asked

Do I need a licence for an 870 FPS rifle?
No. The ZXi stays under 20 J muzzle energy in .177 cal, which keeps it licence-exempt under Arms Rules 2016.
Can I mount a scope?
Yes — it has a standard 3/8″ (11 mm) dovetail rail. Ask us on WhatsApp and we'll bundle a scope that fits your budget.
Spring vs nitro piston — what's the difference?
Springs are simpler and cheaper; nitro pistons (like the Star Matrix Gold) are smoother, quieter and hold cocked without spring fatigue. Both hit the same 870 FPS here.
Which colour will I get?
Six factory colours exist (Army Green, Black, Camo, Carbon Grey, Deep Cobra, Wooden). Confirm live colour stock on WhatsApp before ordering.
